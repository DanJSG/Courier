MongoDB Cheat Sheet:

	Good collection/document viewer:
		Robo3T
	
	Start MongoDB:
		mongod
	
	Start MongoDB shell:
		mongo
	
	Data directory:
		D:\data\db
		
	Start MongoDB with custom data directory:
		mongod --dbpath=<PATH>
		
	To "create" a DB, just type:
		use <DB NAME>
	and then start adding your data.
	
	To add multiple bits of data:
		db.<COLLECTION>.insertMany([
			{foo: "bar", bar: "foo"},
			{foo: "foo", bar: "bar"}
		])
